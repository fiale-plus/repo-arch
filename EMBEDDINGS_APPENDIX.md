# Embeddings in Repo-Arch

Embeddings are connective tissue, not the product moat.

Use them after structured cards, validation, and review are useful. They help with:

- similar past changes
- deduplication
- semantic retrieval
- drift / staleness signals
- evidence linking

Keep structured cards and status as the source of truth.

See [docs/embeddings.md](./docs/embeddings.md).
